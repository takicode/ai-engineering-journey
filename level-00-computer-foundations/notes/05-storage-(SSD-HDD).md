## Storage vs Working Memory

CPU:
- Executes instructions.

RAM:
- Temporary working memory.
- Holds running programs and data.

SSD:
- Persistent storage.
- Holds files, datasets, and programs long-term.

Large storage does not replace large RAM.
Large RAM does not replace large storage.


## Why SSDs Are Non-Volatile

RAM requires continuous power to maintain its state.

SSDs store information in memory cells that can retain trapped electrons even when power is removed.

Therefore, SSDs can preserve data without electricity.
